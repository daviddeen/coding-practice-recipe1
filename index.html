    Scrambled Eggs

    This is the first recipe. Follow these steps

    1. Crack four eggs into a bowl
    2. Use a fork or wisk to mix the eggs together
    3. Pour the eggs into a pan on a stove at medium heat, use a teaspoon of oil in the pan to avoid sticking
    4. Season with salt and pepper, cook to desired consistency
